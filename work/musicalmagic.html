Musical Magic

2018 PROGRAM PARTICIPANT PROJECT

In 2018, I participated in a 1-month program at Intersections Media. We got to learn film production skills and were given a variety of projects to practice them. Musical Magic is one of the many projects I did to gain experience using Final Cut Pro.

Using clips from classic and newly-popular films and tv shows, I edited the music to time with the scene's movements, creating one musical journey of magic!
