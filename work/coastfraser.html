Coast Fraser Product Guide

2019 INTERNSHIP PROJECT

During my internship at Coast Fraser, I had the opportunity to create their new product guide from scratch. This gave me a lot of creative freedom and also the experience I needed as a new designer.

Working on this project, I realized there are a lot of details and challenges that can come from creating a catalog for print. With the collaboration of different departments, I was able to complete the guide and accurately reflect Coast Fraser's products.
