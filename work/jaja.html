JAJA

2019 SCHOOL PROJECT

The final group project I had during my last year at BCIT was creating a website for JAJA. With my two teammates, we created an ecommerce site where the user can create and purchase their own phonecases!

Using Vue.js, Bootstrap, and Firebase, I coded the front-end of JAJA, creating the layout and closely replicating the mockups and wireframes designed by my teammate. Once finished with the front-end, I also got to help out in the back-end.
